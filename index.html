The Challenges Farmers Face

In Ethiopia, smallholder farmers face significant challenges due to limited access to critical resources such as quality seeds, fertilizers, and mechanization. These shortages lead to low productivity, labor-intensive farming, and reduced food security. Without the proper tools and technologies, farmers struggle to overcome these obstacles, making it difficult for them to improve their yields and sustain long-term agricultural growth.

Our Innovative Solutions

An Integrated System

Our solution is an integrated technology transfer system, with Farm Labs serving as local hubs. These hubs provide farmers with access to equipment, digital tools, and resources to improve productivity, profitability, and resilience. By establishing a network of interconnected Farm Labs / Hubs, farmers can share best practices, access market information, and leverage collective bargaining power, driving wider systemic change. Ultimately, this network creates a virtuous cycle of innovation and collaboration, enhancing smallholder farmers' livelihoods and advancing food sovereignty across Ethiopia
